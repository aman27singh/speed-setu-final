Frequently Asked Questions

How often are tracking updates provided?

+

Tracking updates are provided in real-time as your shipment moves through our network. For in-transit shipments, GPS updates are provided every minute. You'll receive notifications at key milestones like pickup, arrival at hub, out for delivery, and delivery.

What if my tracking ID is not working?

+

Make sure you've entered the tracking ID correctly (case-sensitive). If the ID is still not recognized, the shipment may not have been added to our system yet. Please contact our customer support team at info@speedsetu.com or call +91 8884199555 for assistance.

Can I update my delivery address after shipment pickup?

+

For shipments that haven't been picked up yet, you can change the delivery address. Once the shipment has been picked up, changes are limited. Contact our support team immediately with your tracking ID to request any address modifications.

How accurate are the delivery time estimates?

+

Our AI-powered delivery time estimates are accurate within ¬±2 hours based on current traffic conditions, weather, and historical data. We maintain a 98% on-time delivery rate. Actual delivery times may vary based on unforeseen circumstances.

Can I receive SMS and email notifications?

+

Yes! You can enable SMS and email notifications for your shipment. By default, you'll receive notifications at pickup, out for delivery, and delivery stages. You can customize notification preferences in your account settings.

What should I do if my shipment is delayed?

+

If your shipment is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window, our system will automatically notify you. You can also contact our customer support team for assistance. We work diligently to resolve any delays and may provide compensation based on our terms.
