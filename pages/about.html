About Speed Setu

Redefining logistics through technology and reliability

Our Story

Speed Setu was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to transform the logistics landscape of India by bringing speed, reliability, and technology to the forefront of supply chain management.

We recognized a critical gap in the market where small and medium-sized businesses struggled with unreliable, opaque logistics services. Our founders, with their combined 30+ years of experience in supply chain management, set out to create a platform that would be different.

Today, Speed Setu has grown into a trusted logistics company for over 500 businesses, delivering more than 15,000 shipments every month across 50+ cities in India. We continue to innovate and expand our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
