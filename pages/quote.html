What Affects Your Quote?

Several factors influence the final cost of your shipment

1

Distance & Route

The pickup and delivery locations determine the base distance and routing complexity of your shipment.

2

Weight & Dimensions

Heavier items and larger packages may cost more. We charge based on actual weight or dimensional weight.

3

Delivery Urgency

Express deliveries cost more than standard or economy options. Choose based on your timeline.

4

Goods Type

Fragile, hazardous, or temperature-controlled items may have additional handling charges.

5

Volume & Frequency

Bulk shipments or regular volume get special discounts. Ask about our loyalty rates.

6

Insurance & Coverage

Basic insurance is included. Additional coverage options are available for valuable items.

Quote FAQs

How long is a quote valid?

+

Our quotes are valid for 7 days from the date of issue. After that, rates may change based on market conditions. We can provide updated quotes anytime.

Is the quoted price final?

+

The quoted price is final for the shipment details provided. Any changes to weight, dimensions, or delivery location may affect the final cost. We'll inform you of any changes before pickup.

Can I modify my shipment after getting a quote?

+

Yes, you can modify your shipment details. However, changes may affect the quote. Please contact us with any modifications, and we'll provide an updated quote.

Do you offer volume discounts?

+

Absolutely! We offer competitive rates for bulk shipments and regular clients. If you have recurring shipping needs, mention this in your quote request for special pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and checks. We also offer credit terms for regular clients. Details will be provided with your quote.

Can I schedule pickup after getting a quote?

+

Yes, once you accept the quote, we'll help you schedule the pickup at your preferred date and time. We're flexible with scheduling to accommodate your needs.
